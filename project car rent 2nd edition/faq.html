FAQ

QUESTION OUR USER ASKED
1. How do I make a reservation?

You can make a reservation by visiting our website and selecting your desired dates, location, and vehicle type. Follow the prompts to complete the booking process.


2. What documents do I need to rent a car?

You will need a valid driver's license, a credit card in your name, and a form of identification (passport or national ID).


3. Is there an age restriction for renting a car?

Yes, the minimum age for renting a car is 21 years.


4. What is the fuel policy?

Our cars are typically provided with a full tank of gas, and we expect them to be returned in the same condition. Alternatively, you can choose to pre-pay for a full tank of gas at a discounted rate.


5. Can I rent a bike for an extended period?

Yes, we offer long-term bike rentals. Please contact our customer service team for special rates and arrangements.
